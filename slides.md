## Objectives

- Use techniques for solving linear circuits:
  - <span v-mark.underline.red=1>Fourier series and transform</span>
  - Laplace transform
  - Two-port networks
- Model and analyze linear circuits using LTI system concepts
- <span v-mark.underline.red=1>Analyze linear circuits </span> in time and <span v-mark.underline.red=1>frequency domains</span>
- Model and analyze magnetically coupled circuits

---

## Objectives (Cont.)

- Explain terms like:
  - <span v-mark.underline.red=1>Complex frequency</span>, Convolution, <span v-mark.underline.red=1>Frequency response</span>
  - Transfer function, Poles and Zeros, Impulse response
  - <span v-mark.underline.red=1>Parseval theorem, Energy spectral density </span>
  - <span v-mark.underline.red=1>Resonance, Quality factor, Bode plot</span>
  - <span v-mark.underline.red=1>Filters (low-pass, high-pass, etc.)</span>
  - Transformers
